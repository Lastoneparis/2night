Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

2NIGHT (“we”, “us”) builds a location-based social-discovery app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We designed 2NIGHT to collect only what is necessary to help you meet people for real plans tonight.

Information we collect

Account details you provide (name, age, email), your status and plans, approximate and precise location while you are live, profile photos, and messages you send through the app. We also process basic device and usage data to keep the service secure and reliable.

How we use your information

To show you who’s out nearby, deliver the Tonight Feed, enable pings and matches, operate plans and chat, process membership subscriptions, prevent fraud and abuse, and improve the product. We do not sell your personal data.

Location

Location is central to 2NIGHT. You control when you are live. When you set your status, we use your location to surface relevant people near you. You can stop sharing location at any time from your device settings or by going offline in the app.

Sharing

We share data with service providers who help us run the app (hosting, analytics, payments) under contractual safeguards, and where required by law. Other members see only the profile, status, and information you choose to make visible.

Your choices and rights

You can access, correct, export, or delete your account data. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA. To exercise any right, contact us.

Data retention

We keep your data only as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations. When you delete your account, we remove or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period.

Children

2NIGHT is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age required in their jurisdiction.

Cookies and similar technologies

Our website uses a small number of cookies and similar technologies. Essential cookies are required for the site to work and remember your choices, such as your language and your cookie preference. Optional analytics cookies help us understand how the site is used so we can improve it; these are only set if you accept them. You can accept or decline optional cookies in the banner, and change your mind at any time by clearing your browser storage. Declining still lets you use the full site.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at hello@2night.app.
