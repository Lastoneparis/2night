Support

How can we help?

Everything you need to know about 2NIGHT — and a real human at the other end when you need one.

Set your status in one line — what you’re up for tonight. You go live, see who’s out near you in the Tonight Feed, ping the people you like, and match. From there you make a plan and meet — tonight, not tomorrow.

Silver is free forever. Gold is $12.99 per month and unlocks unlimited pings, a 15 km radius, Travel Mode and a weekly Boost. Platinum Black is $29.99 per month and adds a 50 km radius, Frontline visibility, exclusive Black Events and a verified badge.

2NIGHT shows you people within the distance you set — from your street to the whole city. Your tier sets how far the night can reach: Silver 5 km, Gold 15 km, Platinum Black 50 km. It works anywhere in the world, with the people near you wherever you are tonight.

You control when you’re live and what you share. Meet in public, tell a friend your plans, and use good judgement when meeting anyone new. You can block and report anyone in a tap, and our team reviews reports. We don’t run background checks, so your own judgement matters.

Edit your profile, status and visibility any time in the app. To go invisible, simply go offline. You can export or delete your account and data from Settings — when you delete, we remove or anonymise your personal data within a reasonable period.

Gold and Platinum Black are billed through your App Store account and renew automatically until cancelled. Manage or cancel any time in your App Store subscription settings — you’ll keep your benefits until the end of the current billing period. Except where required by law, payments are non-refundable.

We collect only what’s needed to help you meet people tonight, and we never sell your personal data. You choose what’s visible to others and when you share location. Read the full details in our Privacy Policy.
