Safety

Meet people the smart way.

2NIGHT is built for spontaneous, real-life nights. A few simple habits keep them fun — and keep you in control when you meet someone new.

01

Meet in a public place

For a first meet, pick somewhere busy and well-lit — a bar, a café, a popular spot. Stay in public until you genuinely feel comfortable, and don't go to a private location with someone you've just met.

02

Tell a friend your plans

Let someone you trust know where you're going, who you're meeting, and when you expect to be back. Share your live location with them for the night and check in afterwards.

03

Arrange your own transport

Get yourself there and back on your own terms. Keep your phone charged, know how you're getting home, and you're free to leave whenever you want — no explanation needed.

04

Never send money

Never send money, gift cards, or crypto to someone you met on the app, no matter the story. Requests for cash, financial help, or investment "tips" are classic scams — stop and report them.

05

Trust your gut

If something feels off, it probably is. You never owe anyone your time. End the chat, leave the meet, or call a friend — there's no wrong reason to walk away.

06

Report & block

Anyone who makes you uncomfortable can be blocked and reported in a tap. Our team reviews reports — flagging bad behaviour keeps the whole night safer for everyone.

07

Protect your personal info

Keep chats inside 2NIGHT until you trust someone. Don't share your home address, workplace, financial details, or documents, and be thoughtful about photos that reveal exactly where you live.

08

You're in control

You decide when you're live, how far your radius reaches, and what you share. We don't run background checks, so your own judgement always comes first.

In an emergency, call for help.

If you ever feel unsafe or threatened, contact your local emergency services first. To report someone or something to us, email our team — we read every message.
