About 2NIGHT

Tonight, not tomorrow.

2NIGHT is for the moment you decide at 7pm that you actually want to do something — and want real people near you who feel the same.

Most nights die in a group chat. “We should hang out soon.” Soon never comes.

The night you actually go out

2NIGHT was built around one stubborn idea: the best nights aren’t planned three weeks out. They happen because someone, somewhere nearby, is free right now and ready to move. We made the app that turns that impulse into a real plan — fast, in person, tonight.

Set your status in one line. See who’s out near you. Ping, match, meet. No endless swiping toward “maybe next week.” Just the people who are out tonight, and the shortest possible path to being in the same room.

Worldwide, not city-gated

2NIGHT isn’t rolling out one city at a time. It works anywhere on earth — with the people near you, wherever you happen to be tonight. Land in a new city and the night is already live.

You decide how far it reaches. Choose your radius — from your street to the whole city — and 2NIGHT shows you who’s out inside it. Silver keeps it close at 5 km, Gold opens the city at 15 km, Platinum Black goes wide at 50 km.

Travellers

New city, no contacts, one night to make it count. Open 2NIGHT and the local night is already in front of you.

Expats

Building a life somewhere new. Meet people who are out tonight in the place you now call home.

Locals

You know the city — you just want company for tonight. Set your status and find your people, no calendar required.

Designed for the night

True black, gold, and silver. Editorial, quiet, and a little exclusive — built to feel like the best room in the city, not another loud feed. Every screen is made for people who decide late and move fast.
