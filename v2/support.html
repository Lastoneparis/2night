How does 2NIGHT work?

Ping. Match. Meet. Set your radius, ping someone who's out near you, and match the moment it's mutual. Then chat, pick a spot and meet — tonight, not tomorrow.

Where is 2NIGHT available?

Everywhere. 2NIGHT works anywhere on earth and shows you the people nearest you — there's no launch list and no waitlist for your city.

What does "choose your radius" mean?

You decide how far the night reaches, in kilometres. Silver covers 5 km, Gold 15 km and Platinum Black 50 km. 2NIGHT only surfaces people inside the radius you've set.

What's included in each tier?

Silver is free forever — a 5 km radius, 5 pings a day and 1 active plan. Gold ($12.99/month) unlocks a 15 km radius, unlimited pings, Travel Mode and a weekly Boost. Platinum Black ($29.99/month) adds a 50 km radius, Frontline placement, exclusive Black Events and a verified badge.

How is membership billed?

Gold and Platinum Black are billed monthly through your App Store account. You can cancel anytime from your device's subscription settings — your tier stays active until the end of the current billing period.

How do I manage or delete my account?

Open the app, go to your profile settings, and choose Manage Account. From there you can update your details, pause your visibility or permanently delete your account and data.

Still need help?

Email support@2night.co and our team will get back to you. Please include your account email and a short description of what's happening.
