About

Tonight,
not tomorrow.

2NIGHT is the social app for right now. Most apps make you plan for "someday." We built the opposite — a place to find who's out near you and turn an open evening into a real plan, tonight.

Ping. Match. Meet.

Set your radius, ping someone who's free near you, and match the moment it's mutual. Then chat, pick a spot, and meet. It's three steps from your phone to the night — no endless swiping, no waiting weeks for a reply.

Works anywhere on earth.

There's no launch list and no waitlist for your city. Wherever you are, 2NIGHT shows you the people nearest you. You choose how far the night reaches: Silver covers 5 km, Gold 15 km, and Platinum Black 50 km. Your radius, your night.

Three tiers, billed monthly.

Start free with Silver. Go further with Gold at $12.99/month for a 15 km radius, unlimited pings, Travel Mode and a weekly Boost. Own the night with Platinum Black at $29.99/month — a 50 km radius, Frontline placement, exclusive Black Events and a verified badge. Cancel anytime.
